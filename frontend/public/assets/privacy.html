Privacy Policy — Koval

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Controller

Koval Training is operated by Gauthier Robert ("we", "us", "our"). For any privacy-related questions, contact us at gauthier.robert2@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

When you create an account via Google or Strava, we collect:

  • Name, email address, profile picture
  • Unique identifier from the authentication provider (Google ID or Strava athlete ID)

2.2 Training Data

Data you create or import while using the Service:

  • Workouts, training plans, and session history
  • Performance metrics: power, heart rate, cadence, speed, duration
  • Physiological settings: FTP, weight, threshold pace, critical swim speed
  • Performance management data: CTL (fitness), ATL (fatigue), TSB (form)

2.3 Strava Data

When you connect your Strava account, we access the following data with your explicit consent via Strava's OAuth authorization:

  • Activity summaries and time-series data (power, heart rate, cadence, GPS)
  • Athlete profile information

Strava data is used solely to display your own training analysis within the Service. Strava data is cached for a maximum of 7 days and then deleted. We do not share your Strava data with other users, third parties, or use it for advertising. Strava may also collect usage data as described in Strava's Privacy Policy.

2.4 Club and Coach Data

If you join a club or work with a coach, certain data may be visible to club administrators or your coach:

  • Your display name, profile picture, and club membership
  • Session attendance and scheduled workouts within the club

Coach access to your detailed training data requires your explicit consent within the Service.

2.5 Device Data

If you connect Bluetooth fitness devices (trainer, heart rate monitor, power meter, cadence sensor), sensor data is processed locally in your browser and transmitted to our servers only when you save a session.

2.6 FCM Notification Tokens

If you enable push notifications, we store a Firebase Cloud Messaging token linked to your account. This token is used solely to deliver notifications you have opted into.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Cloud Platform (hosting, authentication) — EU region (europe-west1)
  • MongoDB Atlas (database) — data stored in EU
  • Strava API — activity data accessed with your consent, governed by Strava's Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notification delivery
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI-powered workout generation. Only your physiological settings and workout scheduled within Koval are sent. Strava-sourced data is never sent to the AI.

5. Data Retention

  • Account and training data: retained as long as your account is active. Deleted upon account deletion request.
  • Chat history with AI: retained until you delete it or delete your account.
  • FCM tokens: deleted when you disable notifications or delete your account.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., disconnect Strava, disable notifications)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at gauthier.robert2@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Strava Data — Specific Provisions

  • Your Strava data is only displayed to you within the Service, unless you are part of a club classified as a Community Application under the Strava API Agreement.
  • You can disconnect your Strava account at any time from your profile settings. Upon disconnection, all Strava-specific data is deleted from our systems.
  • If you delete an activity on Strava, we will reflect that deletion within 48 hours.
  • We do not sell, license, or transfer your Strava data to any third party.
  • Strava data is never used for AI model training or any machine learning purpose.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Authentication via JWT tokens with secure storage
  • Database access restricted to authenticated services only
  • Strava OAuth tokens are stored securely and never exposed to the frontend

9. International Transfers

Your data is primarily stored and processed in the European Union (Google Cloud europe-west1). Some third-party services (Anthropic, Firebase) may process data in the United States under Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.

10. Children

The Service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the Service. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact
